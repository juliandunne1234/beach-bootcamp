Beach Bootcamps

Introducing Ireland's newest physical and mental endurance challenge. Located on the north side of Dublin city, Dollymount beach is the launch location for Beach Bootcamps. Entry is free as this is part of a community initiative.

On the first Saturday of each month, Bull Island known for its scenic views and long stretches of coastline will be converted into the ultimate all weather assault course. No bootcamp will ever be the same so check out our blog now and suggest a challenge. Hopefully your idea is not what breaks you!
