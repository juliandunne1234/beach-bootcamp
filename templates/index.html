Beach Bootcamps

Introducing Ireland's newest physical and mental endurance challenge. Located on the north side of Dublin city, Dollymount beach is the launch location for Beach Bootcamps. Entry is free as this is part of a community initiative.

Every Bootcamp Changes

Each bootcamp is adapted to include the ideas of the participants. This is an important aspect of each bootcamp. Participants are encouraged to post ideas to the bootcamp blog. The blog is then referenced during planning of each bootcamp. The only part you won't change is the difficulty which will always be maximum!
